# Cloud_Fog_Ship_Detction
Real Cloud and Fog Images in Optical Remote Sensing for Ship Detection. 
## Dataset Description
The dataset have 1646 images, 2796 ships. from 2015 to 2025 years. 

The updated ImCFS dataset comprises 1,879 high-quality images with 3,411 annotated ship instances, curated from Google Earth imagery acquired between 2010 and 2026. It covers major domestic ports in China (Dalian, Guangzhou, Ningbo-Zhoushan, Qingdao, Shanghai and Shenzhen) and key international maritime regions including the Suez Canal, Kiel Canal, Strait of Malacca, as well as ports in Russia (Severomorsk) and the United States (Bremerton). The spatial resolution of the images ranges from 0.22 m to 2.5 m, with image sizes varying from 337 × 271 to 1360 × 911 pixels.
